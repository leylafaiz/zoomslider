This word is pronounced: "afastasqaynakumuhuma". With the arabic language, just adding a letter can completely change the meaning of a word. In this exemple, the first letter "a" implies that a question is going to be asked.

Longest word in english

Floccinaucinihilipilification

This word has 29 letters and means : the act of estimating something as being worth so little as to be practically valueless, or the habit of doing so. It is the longest non-technical, coined word in Oxford Dictionaries of the English language.
